Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our drone services. If you have more questions, don't hesitate to reach out to our team.

Ask a unique question
What is the travel radius?

We cover a 50-mile radius from the city center without travel fees. For interstate projects, a flat travel fee applies.

What if the weather is bad?

Safety is our priority. If it rains or winds exceed 20mph, we reschedule at your earliest convenience at no cost.

Are you FAA certified?

Yes, all our pilots are FAA Part 107 certified and we hold a $2M liability insurance policy for every flight.

How fast is delivery?

Photos are usually delivered within 24 hours. Fully edited 4K cinematic videos take between 48-72 hours.
